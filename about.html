About Us

Welcome to Computer Programming Classes, your trusted partner in building a successful future in the world of technology. Situated in the heart of Kahalgaon at Vishhari Asthan, Thana Road, Choudhary Tola, we are dedicated to providing high-quality education and training in computer programming, web development, and a wide range of IT courses. Our mission is to empower students with practical knowledge, technical skills, and industry-oriented expertise to help them excel in their academic and professional journeys.

Our Vision

At Computer Programming Classes, we envision a world where every student has the opportunity to excel in the field of technology. Our aim is to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical application by providing hands-on experience and real-world project exposure. We strive to create a learning environment that fosters creativity, innovation, and problem-solving skills, ensuring that our students are industry-ready.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert Faculty: Our team of experienced instructors specializes in programming languages, web development, and advanced technologies, ensuring personalized attention for every student.
  • Comprehensive Courses: We offer a wide range of courses including C, C++, Java, Python, C#, ADCA, DCA, and web technologies like front-end and back-end development.
  • Hands-on Learning: Our curriculum is designed to provide practical knowledge through real-world projects, interactive sessions, and coding challenges.
  • Modern Infrastructure: We provide a state-of-the-art computer lab with the latest tools and software to enhance the learning experience.
  • Flexible Timings: Whether you are a student, a working professional, or a beginner, we offer flexible batch timings to fit your schedule.
  • Affordable Fees: Quality education at competitive prices, ensuring that learning is accessible to everyone.
  • Certification: Get recognized with professional certifications for courses like ADCA and DCA to boost your resume.

Our Courses

We specialize in programming and IT courses designed to meet the demands of the modern tech industry. Our key courses include:

  • Programming Languages: Learn C, C++, Java, Python, and C# with practical coding sessions.
  • Web Development: Master front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end technologies to build responsive, dynamic websites.
  • Certification Courses: Become certified in ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications) and DCA (Diploma in Computer Applications).
  • Data Structures & Algorithms: Build a strong foundation in problem-solving techniques using Python.
